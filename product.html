    1.It is another staple fertilizer of organic farming.
    2.It introduces organic matter into the soil and feeds the microbe biome to boost the soil’s fertility and water holding capacity.

    4.The resulting mixture is rich in plant nutrients and beneficial organisms, such as worms and fungal mycelium.
    5.Compost improves soil fertility in gardens, landscaping, horticulture, urban agriculture, and organic farming, reducing dependency on commercial chemical fertilizers.
